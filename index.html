Meet Joshua Idogun

Joshua Idogun is a world-class footballer known for his agility, precision, and tactical intelligence. Born with a passion for the sport, he has risen through the ranks to become one of Nigeria’s most inspiring athletes.

He’s played across continents — with clubs like Green Rangers FC in Nigeria and Fujaira FC in the UAE — leaving a legacy of skill and leadership wherever he steps on the field.

Whether on the national stage or the international arena, John has consistently proven to be a game-changer and a role model to aspiring footballers.
